    Engineering in the New Normal World

    The Covid-19 Pandemic has drastically changed our lives globally. We have to reimagine our lives to what we can call “safe,comfortable living”.Focus areas are specific technology/engineering aspects that fall under the overall Theme. Examples: Seamless integration of Digital Payments into regular transactions, Purification of perishable foods from virus, Use of AI for enforcing social distancing, Building Technologies to quickly build hospital accommodation, etc.How can we use our imagination with engineering knowledge and talents to make significant contribution here – there are lots of opportunities!

    • Focus areas are not limited to those mentioned here; teams can add others under the Theme
    • Projects should clearly define their focus area under the Theme
    • Projects can be in any engineering discipline, or multidisciplinary (e.g.IOT) but should address the Theme using science and technology

    Some Do's

    Participants in the competition should attend all the events – the launch,and other events as and when these would be announced (a video recording of the events will also be made available to all colleges)

    We will be sharing documents and videos pertaining to the last year’s competition with the colleges; all participants may go thru all of them to learn more about the competition

    Note that the last year’s competition format was different from this year’s format

    PIs are requested to keep them available to students until end of competition

    Please follow all the rules and instructions of the competition

    Please submit all the documents within the stated deadline, and in the form asked for – DO NOT MISS ANY DEADLINE

    Share your questions, doubts and comments with us through your innoWAH! Coordinator Ms.C.V.Nisha Angeline,Assistant Professor, IT Dept.

    Some Don’ts

    Participants will be getting various documents, videos etc about the competition from time to time – Please don’t share these with outsiders or others not participating in the competition

    Please don’t upload any competition documents or videos on any website or on social media

    Please don’t copy other people’s ideas and projects from internet or elsewhere 24

    Thiagarajar College of Engineering

    Thiagarajar College of Engineering (TCE), Madurai, an ISO 9001:2008 certified Institution, affiliated to the Anna University is one among the several educational and philanthropic institutions founded by Philanthropist and Industrialist Late. Shri. Karumuttu Thiagarajan Chettiar. It was established in the year 1957 and granted Autonomy in the year 1987. TCE is funded by Central and State Governments and Management.TCE offers Eight Undergraduate Programmes, Thirteen Postgraduate Programmes and Doctoral Programmes in Engineering, Sciences and Architecture.

    About PALS

    PALS association - Actively support Learning Support personnel, Alert members to appropriate resources and professional activities,Establish and maintain links between all bodies involved in learning support,Establish a professional network through all levels of education,Share knowledge of best practice,Provide a representative voice for all learning support personnel.

    About innoWah!

    innoWAH! is an “Innovation Challenge Competition” conducted by PALS every year for its member colleges (Partner Institutions). The Competition is launched at the start of the academic year and concluded by end of February. The prizes will be awarded during the PALS Valediction Function.
